Instructions

Gameplay

Connect 5 squares in a row to win!
Yes, that includes diagonals. You can only place a piece adjacent to an existing square, with the exeption of the very first square, which must be placed in the center. To assist you, "available" squares are a lighter grey color than the locked squares. The boundaries are essentially endless, so a tie is nearly impossible!

Features

Center View = Moves the middle square to the center of your screen.
Username = Just click one to change it! Entering in 'SmartBot3000' will activate the bot and can be in the player 1 or player 2 spot.
Color Picker = Click the color picker to choose a player's square color. Make sure to close the color picker by clicking the button. If you click somewhere else the changes won't take effect until after someone moves.
